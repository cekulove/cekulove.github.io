### \` bbb\"Matrix\" \`
\` A=[[3,-1],[2,0]] \`<br/><br/>
\` B=[[-7,2],[3,5]] \`<br/><br/>
\` A+B=[[3,-1],[2,0]]+[[-7,2],[3,5]]=[[3+(-7),-1+2],[2+3,0+5]]=[[-4,1],[5,5]] \`<br/><br/>
\` AxxB=[[3,-1],[2,0]]xx[[-7,2],[3,5]]=[[3xx(-7)+(-1)xx3,3xx2+(-1)xx5],[2xx(-7)+0xx3,2xx2+0xx5]]=[[-24,1],[-14,4]] \`<br/><br/>

### \` bbb\"Identity Matrix\" \`
\` I_(1)=[1] \`<br/><br/>
\` I_(2)=[[1,0],[0,1]] \`<br/><br/>
\` I_(3)=[[1,0,0],[0,1,0],[0,0,1]] \`<br/><br/>
